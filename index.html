About Me

Hi, I'm Archita!

(she/her)

My love for computer science, which I had initially developed through my enthusiasm for coding, solidified after my team won our first national hackathon, where the app we created was used to assist small businesses. I believe that in this new era of technology, Computer Science can be used as tool of assistance for the needy. I got an excellent opportunity as a research intern in Indian National Center for Ocean Information Services (INCOIS). I was blown away with the extensive use of Computer Science in Oceanography as well as in other branches of sciences. Aiming to hone my skills, I started my graduate studies in PSU. My area of interests are Computer science, Mathematics and gaming. I would like to see myself in a job where there would be a creative fusion between these disciplines. Languages I am good with are C, C++, Java, python, and PostgreSQL. The concepts I am learnt and implemented in projects are Data Structures, OOP, OS, Algorithms, and their computational complexity. If you think that my skills can add value to your company please feel free to reach out!
